Accenture's Learning Series and Recruitment

All graduating CICT students are invited to attend Accenture's Learning Series and Recruitment on March 27, Wednesday, at 8AM in the CICT AVR.

The event will be conducted to provide knowledge and better career opportunities for the graduating CICT students.

The attendees are required to bring their resume and references, an ID, and their best attire for the Interview.

Registration starts at 8AM at the CICT AVR.

Bulacan State University Debate Society

[ANNOUNCEMENT]
This March 30-31, 2019, the Bulacan State University Debate Society will be launching the Bulacan Debate Championship: Revolutionizing Standards at the BulSU Main Campus.

The competition will adhere to the Asian’s Parliamentary Format, which will pit 2 teams of three in the friendly competition which gives the participants a platform to voice out their opinion regarding the path that our society must take regarding many issues.

If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact the signatories below:
Angelica Balatong - President of BSUDS (09552067529)
Miguel Bayani - Vice President of BSUDS, Tournament Director (09358759091)

Cursor Publication Recruitment Requirements

Good day, Cursor applicants!

Are you ready to show what you’ve got and become a Cursor member? You better be, as the recruitment day will be at 8AM on February 9, Saturday inside the CICT AVR-A. Here are the things to bring for the applicants:

Staff Writers: Pen and paper.
Photojournalists: A DSLR or a smartphone with a good quality camera
Cartoonists: Art materials. If you prefer using them, you can bring a pentab and a laptop.
Layout Artists and Video editors: A laptop with editing software.

For those who have classes on Saturday, excuse letters will be provided.

If you want to be a Cursor member but still haven’t submitted an application, the deadline for applications is until today. Just look for us at our booth at the 3rd floor of CICT or inside the Student Center at the 4th floor.

See you there and good luck!
