Bulacan State University Debate Society
[ANNOUNCEMENT]

This March 30-31, 2019, the Bulacan State University Debate Society will be launching the Bulacan Debate Championship: Revolutionizing Standards at the BulSU Main Campus.

The competition will adhere to the Asian’s Parliamentary Format, which will pit 2 teams of three in the friendly competition which gives the participants a platform to voice out their opinion regarding the path that our society must take regarding many issues.

If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact the signatories below: Angelica Balatong - President of BSUDS (09552067529) Miguel Bayani - Vice President of BSUDS, Tournament Director (09358759091)

Commission on Student Elections
Have you always wanted to serve the University, but, you don't know how? Wonder no more as the University's Commission on Student Elections (CSE) is looking for a new CICT Commissioner!

Academic/University Requirements: Will be enrolling this 2nd Semester, A.Y. 2018-2019;
• preferrably non-graduating.
• With good academic standing (No Grades of 3.00 below)
• If an officer of any duly recognized university organization, the student must tender a leave from his/her organization within the election period.
• Definitely not affiliated with any student political groups in the University Character

Skills Requirements:
• Has a character of a good leader
• Excellent in analyzing situations
• With high moral background
• Has a positive character to inspire and lead by example
• Has a keen attention to details and can work with minimal instructions
• Knows how to take responsibility and has the passion to serve
• Creative and innovative in decision making
• Communications Skills: excellent both in Filipino/English grammar

NOTE: The commissioner must have intermediate skills in graphic design.

Think you got the mettle to be a Commissioner? Then, please contact: CSER Comm. Dianne Jose 0997-687-9271 Head Committee on Secretariat Applications will be accepted until February 1, 2019 only. Submit your applications, mga ka-Cursor!
Cursor Publication Recruitment
Cursor Publication conducted a Recruitment yesterday wherein the aspiring Cursor staff writers, photojournalists, layout artists, cartoonists, and video editors put their talents to the test for a chance to become the publication's newest member.
Cursor Publication Recruitment Requirements
Good day, Cursor applicants!

Are you ready to show what you’ve got and become a Cursor member? You better be, as the recruitment day will be at 8AM on February 9, Saturday inside the CICT AVR-A. Here are the things to bring for the applicants:

Staff Writers: Pen and paper.

Photojournalists: A DSLR or a smartphone with a good quality camera

Cartoonists: Art materials. If you prefer using them, you can bring a pentab and a laptop.

Layout Artists and Video editors: A laptop with editing software.

For those who have classes on Saturday, excuse letters will be provided.

If you want to be a Cursor member but still haven’t submitted an application, the deadline for applications is until today. Just look for us at our booth at the 3rd floor of CICT or inside the Student Center at the 4th floor.

See you there and good luck!

Free seminars in Video Editing (Premiere Pro)
Having trouble designing your projects and editing video documentaries? Be stressed no more. Excuse letters and Certificates will be provided for all participants
